About LanguageSwap

  • Why should you use languageSwap when there are so many apps and tools to learn languages?

    LanguageSwap allows you to meet real people and create a connection that will help you when you are struggling with the challenges of learning a new language. Plus, we know that sometimes people are very worried of making mistakes when learning a new language, so meeting someone who is also trying to learn a language may help you understand that everyone struggles at something!

  • Is LanguageSwap free?

    Yes, of course! You will meet many people who are trying to learn like you, so nobody should charge you as if it was a tutoring lesson!
